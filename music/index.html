Florence + The Machine share majestic video for new single ‘Hunger’ and confirm ‘High As Hope’ album release date

3 March 2018

Having recently dropped new track ‘Sky Full Of Song‘ for Record Store Day 2018, Florence Welch and co have now confirmed that new album ‘High As Hope’ will be released on June 29. Welch worked with the likes of Jamie xx, Kamasi Washington, Sampha, Tobias Jesso Jr and Kelsey Lu on the release.
